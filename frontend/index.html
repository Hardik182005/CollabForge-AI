The live intelligence pipeline

Anakin gathers public data, structures the evidence and feeds every CollabForge decision.

Runs against live Anakin sources only when you click — nothing is pre-loaded or hardcoded.

How it works

Input → Anakin → structured evidence → reasoning → decision.

01

Route & retrieve

A deterministic source router picks Anakin Wire for structured platform actions, the Universal Scraper for public pages, and Search for open web — in that order.

02

Normalize evidence

Every result becomes a cited evidence record with its source, provider and confidence. Duplicates are removed; raw pages never reach your browser.

03

Reason & build

Only after evidence is collected does the model reason over it — producing explainable scores, outreach, briefs and contracts you can edit.

CollabForge AI

Research. Score. Create. Close.

CollabForge surfaces only public data through Anakin. Scores are explainable estimates, not guarantees. Contracts are AI-generated templates for review, not legal advice.
